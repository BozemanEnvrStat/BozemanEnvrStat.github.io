Bozeman Environmental and Ecological Statistics Research Group

Investigating statistical research in the context of environmental and ecological applications.

Current Members

We are a collection of statisticians in the Bozeman, Montana area that includes government statisticians at the US Geological Survey's (USGS) Northern Rocky Mountain Science Center (NOROCK), Montana State University (MSU) faculty and students, and others that work on statistical research in environmental and ecological settings.
